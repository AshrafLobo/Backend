The Board of Directors of WPP Scangroup plc wishes to inform its shareholders and the public that there is an ongoing delay in the publication of the audited financial statements for the financial year ended 31st December 2020 which were due on 30th April 2021. This has been occasioned by ongoing investigations previously announced following management changes at the Company. The Board will provide an update for the date of the publication of the financial results by 31st May 2021, and that the date for the Company's annual general meeting will be announced at the same time.
